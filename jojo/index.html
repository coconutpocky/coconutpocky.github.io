#10

Tonio Trussardi

Unknown to may fans of the series, Tonio was the sole reason that Josuke and friends were able to defeat Yoshikage Kira. Without Tonio's stand Pearl Jam, Josuke and Okuyasu would never have felt refreshed enough to confront Kira. Tonio is also a great character because he is one of the only people in the entire series to use his stand for selfless reasons.

#9

Robert E.O. Speedwagon

Without a doubt the most important side character in the entire series. The protagonists of series 2, 3, and 4 would have been doomed without funding from the Speedwagon Foundation. Not only does Robert have one of the best names in the series he also uses his immense wealth to help the wellbeing of the entire world through medical, historical, and technological advances. Also his hat is awesome.

#8

Kars

One of the strongest villains of the entire series. If it had not been for dumb luck on Joesephs part, it is very likely that Kars would have killed everyone and became a living god. Unfortunately, he is now a rock floating through space. R.I.P.

#7

Noriaki Kakyoin

Kakyoin may not be the most powerful among the group, but his quick thinking and tenacity were the sole reason that Jotaro and friends were able to defeat DIO. He also has one of the greatest scenes in the anime. ReroReroRero

#6

Josuke Higashikata

One of my favorite character/stand combo in the series, Josuke and Crazy diamond are very powerful without being overpowered. The fact that Josuke is unable to use Crazy Diamond on himself really emphasizes how heroic and tenacious he can be when jumping into dangerous situations.

#5

Joseph Joestar

Creator of the famous Joestar backup plan, Joseph is one of the most clever characters in the series. He is also the only character shown who can use both Hamon and a stand. In addition, he is the only character to have a role in three seperate JOJO series.

#4

Jotaro Kujo

OP stand. Never loses his cool. The only person who is strong enough to fight DIO.

#3

Yoshikage Kira

The second best villain the series. This guy has so many stand powers that it's a miracle that Josuke and friends were able to corner him in the first place.

#2

Rohan Kishibe

Rohan and his stand Heavens Door are one of the most powerful character/stand combinations in the series. Not only is Rohan highly intelligent and agile, his stand can theoretically make anything happen if used correctly.

#1

DIO

Objectively the best character in all of JOJO. MUDA MUDA MUDA MUDA MUDA MUDA!
